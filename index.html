I'm currently a bioinformatics PhD candidate in Julie Hussien's lab at the Montreal Heart Institute, affiliated with the Université de Montréal. Before this, I completed my Masters in Cellular and Molecular Biology with something something Côté at the University of Ottawa.

My research applies high-throughput experimental systems and single-cell genomics to understand how cellular plasticity—the ability of a cell to adapt to its environment—contributes to tumour progression and tissue regeneration.

Beyond this work, I'm passionate about training and producing resources for the scientific community, from day-long workshops on introductory scRNA-seq analysis to code snippets for common steps in exploratory data analysis. A current project is to develop resources for personal knowledge management in science.

Please feel free to reach out to discuss collaborations, workshops, or to chat about anything else!
